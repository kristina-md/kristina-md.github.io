The Power of Pets

Nothing compares to the joy of coming home to a loyal companion. The unconditional love of a pet can do more than keep you company. Pets may also decrease stress, improve heart health, and even help children with their emotional and social skills.

Scientists are looking at what the potential physical and mental health benefits are for different animals—from fish to guinea pigs to dogs and cats.

If you want to know more about the effect pets have on us, you can read more here.

About PetShelter

About Us

PetShelter was formed in 2001 with the purpose of helping animals find a second home. Each of them has lots of love to give if they are only given a chance.

Would you like to be the one to give them a home?
Our Pets

Our pets are all vaccinated and ready for a new home.
Some of them need special atention or further socialisation efforts, but they will be pointed out to you. We recruit the help of volunteers willing to help, and we are constantly working on their rehabilitation.
